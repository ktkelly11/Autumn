Autumn

The very best season!

Autumn, also known as Fall in the United States, is one of four seasons. Autumn marks the transition from summer to winter, starting in September and ending in December. Autumn is the season when the duration of daylight becomes noticeably shorter and the temperature cools considerably. In the USA the leaves change to reds, oranges, and yellows and fall off of trees. Pumpkins and apples are harvested. It is marked by two holidays in the USA, Halloween, when children go trick-or-treating, and Thanksgiving, when families gather to eat a meal and talk about what they are most thankful for.
